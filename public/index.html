How Our Fraud Detection Works

Secure, privacy-preserving technology that protects all stakeholders

1

Applicant Data Hashing

When a loan application is submitted, your system generates a unique cryptographic hash of key identifiers (name, DOB, ID numbers). Actual PII never leaves your environment.

2

Secure Database Query

The encrypted hash is sent securely to our system, where we check against our consortium database of recent applications from participating financial institutions.

3

Matching Algorithm

Our proprietary matching technology identifies if similar hashes have been submitted recently. The algorithm accounts for variations while preventing false positives.

4

Instant Risk Assessment

Within milliseconds, we return a confidence score indicating potential loan stacking. Your team receives actionable insights without exposing sensitive data.

Secure Data Exchange

Our technology ensures that no sensitive applicant data is ever exposed or centralized. This privacy-preserving approach meets the highest security standards while providing powerful fraud protection.
